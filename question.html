Frequently Asked Questions:


How do I create an account at the Bank of Musa?
It's really simple! In our navigation bar above select the Register option. It will take you to our registration form where you will need to fill out your personal information details including, full name, date of birth, address, social security number, and more.
How can I access my ATM and withdraw?
When you first register to our bank, upon validation, you should immediately receive an email that contains your own personal PIN number. Using this PIN, when you log into your user dashboard, you can navigate to the ATM page. From there you'll be free to do any functionality you desire!
For business and personal needs, does Bank of Musa allow opening both checkings and savings accounts?
Absolutely! We understand that our customers have different needs whether business or personal. When creating an account, users have the option to open as many checkings and/or savings accounts as they need. These accounts receive their own account number and are displayed on the user dashboard. Users are free to view each account details as well and monitor the transactions between the accounts as well.
What security measures does the Bank of Musa implement to ensure the safety of customers' accounts and personal details?
Bank of Musa puts our customers' privacy and security first and foremost. When users log in, they are required to go through the implemented two-factor authentication system set in place where an authentication key is sent to the user's email. Users are required to input this authentication key or users won't be permitted to log into their account.
Is there anyway I can keep track of the transactions on all my accounts?
Yes, absolutely! If you notice on the bottom of the user dashboard, there is a 'Show Transactions' button that displays all the transactions within your account along with its timestamp. It is ordered from most recent to earliest. This way users can keep track of all the transactions on their various accounts and if there is any irregular or suspicious activity you notice within your transaction history, feel free to contact our admin through our 'Contact Us' page and notify us of the issue.
How am I able to electronically deposit checks to the bank?
At Bank of Musa, we electronically accept checks! On your user dashboard, navigate to the 'Check Deposit' page on the navbar. Once on the page, upload the electronically scanned check, making sure that its an 'IMG' type file. Specify the amount on the check and submit. All the check images are stored within our secure database.
